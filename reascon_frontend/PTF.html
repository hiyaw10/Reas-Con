PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The Ethiopian Airlines Pilot Training Facility is a state-of-the-art construction project aimed at creating a premier training center for aspiring aviators. Situated on a sprawling 50-acre site, this facility will feature cutting-edge flight simulators, classrooms, hangars, and a dormitory for trainees. The primary goal is to provide comprehensive pilot training for both civilian and military aviation, equipping the next generation of aviators with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the skies safely and efficiently. The project includes the construction of runways, control towers, and maintenance facilities, offering a holistic training environment for all levels of aviation enthusiasts.
